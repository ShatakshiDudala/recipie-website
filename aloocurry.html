Simple Aloo Curry

Ingredients

  • 3–4 medium potatoes (peeled & cubed)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1½ tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Water as needed

Procedure

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them crackle.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Stir in tomatoes and cook until they soften and release oil.
  5. Add turmeric, red chili, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add potato cubes and sauté for 3–4 minutes to coat them in masala.
  7. Pour enough water to cover the potatoes. Cover and cook on low flame for 15–20 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala, simmer for another 2–3 minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
