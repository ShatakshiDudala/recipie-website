Street-Style Chicken Roll

Ingredients

For the Wrap

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour (or maida)
  • Water to knead
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp oil

For the Chicken Filling

  • 200g boneless chicken (cut into small pieces)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp cumin powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • Salt to taste

For Assembly

  • 1 small onion (thinly sliced)
  • Chaat masala (optional)
  • Green chutney or mayonnaise
  • Butter or oil for cooking paratha

Procedure

1. Prepare the Dough

  1. Mix flour, salt, and water. Knead into a soft dough and let it rest for 20 minutes.

2. Cook the Chicken

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until aromatic.
  2. Add chicken, turmeric, chili, cumin, salt, and garam masala.
  3. Cook on medium heat until chicken is done and slightly crispy. Add lemon juice. Mix and set aside.

3. Make the Wraps

  1. Roll dough into thin circles (roti size).
  2. Cook on a hot tawa with some oil or butter until golden on both sides.

4. Assemble the Rolls

  1. Place cooked wrap on a flat surface. Spread chutney or mayo over it.
  2. Add chicken filling, top with sliced onions, and a sprinkle of chaat masala.
  3. Roll tightly, wrap in foil or butter paper, and serve hot!
