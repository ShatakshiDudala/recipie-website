Spiced Paneer Roll

Ingredients

For the Wrap

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour (atta)
  • 1 tsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water to knead

For the Paneer Filling

  • 200g paneer (cubed or crumbled)
  • 1 onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1 capsicum (thinly sliced)
  • 1 tsp oil or butter
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp chili powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • ½ tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Lemon juice and fresh coriander (for garnish)

Optional for Assembly

  • Green chutney
  • Mayonnaise or hung curd
  • Pickled onions or lettuce

Procedure

1. Prepare the Dough

  1. Knead flour, salt, and water with oil into a soft dough. Rest for 20 minutes.

2. Cook the Filling

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds.
  2. Once they sizzle, add onions and sauté until golden.
  3. Add capsicum and spices. Stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  4. Add paneer and gently mix. Cook for 3–4 minutes.
  5. Turn off the heat. Add lemon juice and chopped coriander.

3. Prepare the Wraps

  1. Divide dough into balls and roll into thin rotis.
  2. Roast on a hot tawa with light oil or butter until golden on both sides.

4. Assemble the Rolls

  1. Place the roti on a plate. Spread green chutney or mayo if using.
  2. Add a portion of the paneer filling and top with salad or pickles.
  3. Roll tightly and wrap in foil or butter paper. Serve hot or pack to-go!
