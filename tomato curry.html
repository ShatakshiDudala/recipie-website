South Indian Style Tomato Curry

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes (chopped or pureed)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 green chili (slit)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1½ tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Curry leaves (optional)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Procedure

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add curry leaves and green chili, followed by chopped onions. Sauté until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Add turmeric, chili, and coriander powders. Stir briefly.
  5. Add chopped or pureed tomatoes and cook on medium heat until oil separates (about 8–10 minutes).
  6. Season with salt. Simmer and stir occasionally for desired consistency (add water if needed).
  7. Garnish with chopped coriander and serve hot with rice, dosa, or roti!
