Street-Style Veg Roll

Ingredients

For the Wrap

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • Water as needed to knead dough
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp oil

For the Filling

  • 1 boiled potato (mashed)
  • ½ cup shredded cabbage
  • ½ cup grated carrot
  • ¼ cup capsicum (julienned)
  • 1 small onion (sliced)
  • 1 green chili (optional)
  • ½ tsp chaat masala
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tsp oil

Procedure

1. Prepare the Dough

  1. Knead flour with water, salt, and a little oil into a soft dough. Rest for 15–20 minutes.

2. Make the Filling

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add onions and sauté until translucent.
  2. Add all veggies and sauté on high flame for 4–5 minutes until slightly tender but crunchy.
  3. Add mashed potato, spices, lemon juice, and salt. Mix well. Let it cool.

3. Cook the Wraps

  1. Divide dough into balls and roll into thin chapatis.
  2. Roast on a hot tawa with a touch of oil until golden on both sides.

4. Assemble the Rolls

  1. Place some filling in the center of the wrap.
  2. Optionally drizzle ketchup, mayo, or green chutney.
  3. Roll tightly, wrap in foil or paper, and serve hot or pack for later!
