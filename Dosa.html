Classic South Indian Dosa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 3 cups parboiled rice
  • ¼ tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee (for cooking)

Procedure

  1. Soak urad dal and fenugreek seeds together in water for 5–6 hours.
  2. Soak rice in water separately for the same duration.
  3. Grind dal and rice separately into a smooth batter, then mix them together.
  4. Let the batter ferment overnight or for 8–10 hours in a warm place.
  5. Add salt and stir the batter before making dosas.
  6. Heat a flat pan (tawa), pour a ladle of batter, and spread it in a circular motion.
  7. Drizzle oil or ghee around the edges. Cook until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Fold and serve hot with chutney or sambar!
