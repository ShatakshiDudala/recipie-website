Veg Hakka Noodles

Ingredients

  • 200g Hakka noodles
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp garlic (minced)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 cup cabbage (shredded)
  • 1 carrot (julienned)
  • ½ cup capsicum (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp green chili sauce (optional)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Procedure

  1. Boil noodles as per packet instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water. Toss with a little oil to prevent sticking.
  2. Heat oil in a wok. Add garlic and sauté for 30 seconds.
  3. Add onion, cabbage, carrot, and capsicum. Stir fry on high heat for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Add soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce, salt, and pepper.
  5. Add boiled noodles. Toss everything together well.
  6. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot!
