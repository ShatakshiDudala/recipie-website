Hyderabadi Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken (marinated with yogurt, spices)
  • 1 cup fried onions
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, etc.)
  • Fresh mint and coriander leaves
  • Saffron soaked in milk
  • Ghee and oil
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  1. Parboil the rice with whole spices. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, layer marinated chicken, then rice, then fried onions, herbs, and saffron milk.
  3. Seal the pot and cook on dum (low heat) for 30–40 minutes.
  4. Let it rest for 10 minutes, then fluff and serve hot with raita!
