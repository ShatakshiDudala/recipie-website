Classic Homemade Veg Burger

Ingredients

For the Patty

  • 2 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • ¼ cup grated carrot
  • ¼ cup boiled peas or corn
  • 2 tbsp breadcrumbs
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for shallow frying

For Assembly

  • 2 burger buns
  • Butter or mayonnaise
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion rings
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Ketchup or mustard

Procedure

  1. Make the Patty: Mix mashed potatoes, grated carrot, peas, breadcrumbs, and spices. Shape into round patties.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and shallow-fry the patties until golden brown and crisp on both sides.
  3. Toast the Buns: Lightly toast the inside of the burger buns on a hot tawa with some butter.
  4. Assemble: Spread mayonnaise or butter on buns. Place lettuce, a patty, cheese slice (if using), tomato, and onion.
  5. Add ketchup or mustard as desired. Top with the other half of the bun.
  6. Serve hot with fries or a side salad!
