Spicy Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (bone-in or boneless, cut into pieces)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed or chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • ½ cup yogurt (optional for richness)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1½ tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1½ tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Water as needed for gravy

Procedure

  1. Heat oil in a large pan. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute until aromatic.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft and oil separates from the mixture.
  4. Stir in turmeric, chili, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add chicken pieces and sauté until coated and sealed (about 5–7 minutes).
  6. Optional: Stir in yogurt and cook for 2 minutes to add richness.
  7. Pour in water for desired gravy consistency. Cover and cook on medium heat for 20–25 minutes or until chicken is tender.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and simmer for another 2–3 minutes.
  9. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.
