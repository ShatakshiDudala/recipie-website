Masala Maggi

Ingredients

  • 1 packet instant noodles (Maggi or similar)
  • 1½ cups water
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • ½ tomato (chopped)
  • ¼ cup capsicum or green peas (optional)
  • 1 green chili (optional)
  • ½ tsp garam masala or extra chili powder (optional)
  • 1 tsp oil or butter
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish)

Procedure

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Sauté onions until golden, then add tomato and cook until soft.
  2. Add green chili and vegetables (if using). Cook for 2–3 minutes.
  3. Pour in water. Once it starts boiling, add the noodle masala (from the packet) and mix well.
  4. Add noodles and cook on medium heat until soft (about 2–3 minutes), stirring occasionally.
  5. Sprinkle garam masala if you'd like more flavor. Mix and simmer for another minute.
  6. Top with fresh coriander and serve hot!
