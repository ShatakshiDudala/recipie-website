Delicious Paneer Curry

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chili (optional)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1½ tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp fresh cream or cashew paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Procedure

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Mix in ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1 minute.
  4. Add tomato puree, green chili, and cook until oil starts to separate.
  5. Sprinkle turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Add a little water to adjust consistency and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Add paneer cubes and stir gently. Simmer for 3–4 minutes.
  8. Stir in fresh cream or cashew paste. Sprinkle garam masala and cook for 1 minute.
  9. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with roti, naan, or jeera rice.
