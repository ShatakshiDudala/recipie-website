Homemade Veg Pizza

Ingredients

For the Dough

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (or any cooking oil)

For the Toppings

  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • ½ cup mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Sliced onion, tomato, capsicum, and olives
  • Chili flakes, oregano (optional)

Procedure

1. Prepare the Dough

  1. In a bowl, mix yeast, sugar, and warm water. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes to activate.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead into a soft dough.
  3. Cover and let it rise for 1–2 hours until doubled in size.

2. Assemble the Pizza

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). Grease a baking tray or pizza pan.
  2. Punch down dough and roll it out to desired thickness. Place on the tray.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly. Add cheese and your favorite toppings.
  4. Sprinkle chili flakes or oregano if desired.

3. Bake and Serve

  1. Bake in the preheated oven for 12–15 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  2. Cool for 2 minutes, slice, and enjoy hot!
