Rich & Creamy Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (penne or fettuccine)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chili flakes and oregano (optional)
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)

Procedure

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and sauté garlic and onions until translucent.
  3. Pour in cream and milk. Stir and simmer on low heat for 3–4 minutes.
  4. Add parmesan cheese, salt, pepper, chili flakes, and oregano. Stir until creamy.
  5. Add cooked pasta to the sauce and toss until well coated.
  6. Let it simmer for 2 more minutes so the flavors infuse.
  7. Garnish with parsley and serve hot!
