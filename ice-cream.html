Homemade Vanilla Ice Cream (No Machine)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream (chilled)
  • 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1½ tsp vanilla extract
  • Optional: chocolate chips, chopped nuts, or fruit puree

Procedure

  1. In a chilled bowl, whip the heavy cream using a hand mixer or whisk until soft peaks form.
  2. In another bowl, mix condensed milk and vanilla extract.
  3. Gently fold the whipped cream into the condensed milk mixture. Do not overmix.
  4. If using, add chocolate chips, nuts, or puree and fold in gently.
  5. Transfer the mixture into a lidded container and smooth the top.
  6. Cover and freeze for at least 6–8 hours or overnight until firm.
  7. Scoop and serve with toppings of your choice!
