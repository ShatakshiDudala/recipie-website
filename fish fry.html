Spicy South Indian Fish Fry

Ingredients

  • 500g fish fillets (rohu, tilapia, king fish, or your choice)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala or fish masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice or vinegar
  • 2 tbsp rice flour or semolina (for crispiness)
  • 2–3 tbsp oil for shallow frying

Procedure

  1. Clean and pat dry the fish pieces using a paper towel.
  2. In a bowl, mix all spices, salt, lemon juice, and ginger-garlic paste into a thick marinade.
  3. Coat fish pieces well with the marinade. Sprinkle rice flour or semolina over them.
  4. Let the fish marinate for at least 30 minutes (up to 2 hours in the fridge for deeper flavor).
  5. Heat oil in a flat pan or skillet on medium flame.
  6. Gently place the fish and fry 3–4 minutes per side until crisp and golden.
  7. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with lemon wedges and onion slices!
